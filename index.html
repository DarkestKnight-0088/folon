🚀 Folon Marketing Funnel Overview

Strategic Framework Purpose

The Folon Marketing Funnel is a comprehensive 11-month strategic implementation plan designed to systematically acquire 100,000 app downloads and convert them into revenue-generating active users.

Key Objectives:

  • Primary Goal: 100,000 realistic app downloads
  • Secondary Goal: 21,000 registered users (21% conversion)
  • Tertiary Goal: 6,930 active users (33% engagement rate)
  • Revenue Goal: 285 loan applications with sustainable monetization

Data-Driven Foundation:

Based on comprehensive market research including Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics data, GSMA Mobile Connectivity Index, and iFarmer internal analytics. Target represents 5.7% penetration of operational area smartphone users.

📊 Executive Summary Details

Strategic Approach

The funnel employs a district-prioritized approach focusing resources on high-potential areas while maintaining presence across all operational districts.

Market Penetration Calculation:

Target Downloads: 100,000
Available Market: 1.74M smartphone users
Penetration Rate: 100,000 ÷ 1,740,000 = 5.7%

Conservative Economic Model:

  • Customer LTV: BDT 3,500 over 12 months
  • Customer CAC: BDT 1,285 for active users
  • Payback Period: 4.4 months
  • Monthly Revenue per Customer: BDT 292
